Shipping & Orders

How to Order

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click the cart icon and proceed to checkout
  3. Fill in your details and submit your order
  4. We'll confirm your order via email
  5. Pay via Swish (details sent in confirmation email)
  6. We handmake and ship your order!

Delivery

  • Delivery time: 2–3 business days after payment
  • Delivery area: Sweden only
  • Shipping: Via PostNord

Payment

We accept payment via Swish. After you place your order, we'll send you a confirmation email with our Swish number and the total amount.

Questions?

Contact us anytime at root27814@gmail.com or through our contact page.
