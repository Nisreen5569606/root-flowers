About Root

Our Story

Root was born out of a love for creating beautiful things by hand. Based in Sweden, we craft unique pipe cleaner flower bouquets and handmade flower candle arrangements that bring colour and joy into any space.

Every single piece is made by hand with care and attention to detail. No two bouquets are exactly alike — each one is a little work of art.

What We Make

  • Pipe Cleaner Flower Bouquets — Colourful, everlasting flowers that never wilt
  • Flower Candle Bouquets — Beautiful handmade arrangements combining flowers and candles

Why Root?

  • 100% handmade with love
  • Unique, one-of-a-kind pieces
  • Perfect gifts for any occasion
  • Flowers that last forever
  • Based in Sweden, delivering across the country
