About Root

Our Story

Hi, I'm Nisreen — a 26-year-old from Iraq, now living in Sweden. By day I work with programming and coding, but I needed something to break the routine and add colour to my everyday life.

It started with pipe cleaner flowers — twisting, shaping, and bringing little blooms to life. The idea grew, and before long I was making candle bouquets too. What began as a creative escape became something much more meaningful.

My mum and I create everything together. We love putting our personal touch into each piece — it's our shared art, our way of spreading a little beauty into the world.

What We Make

  • Pipe Cleaner Flower Bouquets — Colourful, everlasting flowers that never wilt
  • Flower Candle Bouquets — Beautiful handmade arrangements combining flowers and candles
