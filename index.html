Our Atelier

Born from a need for colour

I'm Nisreen, a programmer from Iraq living in Sweden. After long days of coding, I needed something to add colour and creativity to my routine — so I started sculpting flowers from textile. The idea grew, and soon candles followed.

My mum and I create everything together. We love putting our touch into art, making each piece a little reflection of us. Root is our shared joy — handmade with love, one bloom at a time.
