Our Heart

Born from a need for colour

I'm Nisreen, a 26-year-old programmer from Iraq living in Sweden. After long days of coding, I needed something to add colour and creativity to my routine — so I started making pipe cleaner flowers. The idea grew, and soon candles followed.

My mum and I create everything together. We love putting our touch into art, making each piece a little reflection of us. Root is our shared joy — handmade with love, one bloom at a time.
